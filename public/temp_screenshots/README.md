# CRM Screenshots

Please place the screenshots for the CRM modules in this folder. For best results, use the following filenames:

1. `pipeline.png` - Pipeline board/Leads drag-and-drop
2. `leads.png` - Lead list & lead detail views
3. `conversations.png` - SMS Chat & Inbox
4. `calls.png` - Voice dialing & call log history
5. `calendar.png` - Google Calendar sync and appointment scheduler
6. `automations.png` - SMS Sequences & AI Re-engage
7. `ai_chat.png` - RevRa AI Chatbot
8. `marketplace.png` - Lead Marketplace & Pools
9. `analytics.png` - CRM Analytics/Dashboard
10. `settings.png` - Settings and integrations

The Python script will automatically detect files in this directory and embed them into the generated PDF documentation.
